About David

David started learning web design and programming in the middle of 2022 after deciding that it was a suitable career path for an ever growing digital world

He has a degree in Illustration with Animation, majoring in drawing and narrative illustration for his final year project. He has a keen eye for composition and how to effectively layout a page, online or in an analogue space.

This site was built to showcase three projects, each of which came from a different design brief, and to showcase the skills that he has acquired since developing those three websites.
